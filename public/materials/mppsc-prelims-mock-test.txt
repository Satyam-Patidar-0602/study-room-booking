MPPSC PRELIMS MOCK TEST 2024
===========================

GENERAL STUDIES
---------------

Q1. Who was the first woman Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh?
(a) Uma Bharti
(b) Sushma Swaraj
(c) Vijaya Raje Scindia
(d) Kamalapati Tripathi
Answer: (a) Uma Bharti

Q2. The Tropic of Cancer passes through how many districts of Madhya Pradesh?
(a) 8
(b) 14
(c) 10
(d) 12
Answer: (b) 14

Q3. Which river is known as the 'Lifeline of Madhya Pradesh'?
(a) Narmada
(b) Tapti
(c) Betwa
(d) Chambal
Answer: (a) Narmada

Q4. Who is the author of the book 'Discovery of India'?
(a) Mahatma Gandhi
(b) Jawaharlal Nehru
(c) Sardar Patel
(d) Subhash Chandra Bose
Answer: (b) Jawaharlal Nehru

Q5. The largest tribal group in Madhya Pradesh is:
(a) Bhil
(b) Gond
(c) Baiga
(d) Korku
Answer: (b) Gond

---

ANSWER KEY:
1. (a)
2. (b)
3. (a)
4. (b)
5. (b)
